# Product Hunt

**Name:** Save Image as Any Type

**Tagline:** Right-click any web image. Save as PNG, JPG, or TIFF. Done.

**Description:**

Tired of saving images from the web and getting .webp files you can't use?

Save Image as Any Type adds a simple right-click menu to every image in Chrome. Pick PNG, JPG, or TIFF and the Save As dialog appears instantly.

**Why this exists:**
Websites now serve images in WebP and AVIF — formats that many tools, email clients, and workflows still don't support. Instead of downloading and converting with another app, just right-click and choose your format.

**Key details:**
- Converts any browser-renderable image (WebP, AVIF, SVG, GIF, PNG, JPG)
- JPG export automatically handles transparency (white fill, no black backgrounds)
- TIFF support for print and professional workflows
- All conversion happens locally in your browser
- Zero data collection, zero accounts, zero ads
- Uses UTIF.js from the creator of Photopea for TIFF encoding
